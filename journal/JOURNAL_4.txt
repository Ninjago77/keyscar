Now I don't have any major errors, so time to move on!

The following are the footprints I'm using:
 - Capacitor SMD:C 0402 1005 Metric
 - SS12's SMA (I had to swap it out from a tht one, so this is actually smt)
 - Neopixel SMD:LED WS2812B PLCC4 5.0x5.Omm P3.2mm
 - USB A Plug: USB_A_Molex_48037-2200_Horizontal
 - resistors: R_0402_1005Metric

I seem to have run into a problem regarding the buttons,
I want the bootsel and reset buttons to be incredibly small,
and the exec buttons to be medium, but finding the footprints for these 
small buttons (3mmx3mm) is pretty hard. Most always give me 6mm footprints.

So, I think, I have to base my footprints off of the actual parts in stock
with JCLPCB. So, here are the 2 buttons that I've picked:-
 - EVQP2D02Q for the bootsel and reset buttons
 - K2-6639SP-C4SC-04 for the EXEC buttons
 - K3-1292S-P2 for the spdt switches
 - GT36516 for the photoresistor
